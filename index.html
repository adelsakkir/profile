About Me

Hi there, I am Adel Sakkir. I am currently a student of IIT, Kharagpur. I spent half my childhood and schooling in Dubai, UAE after which I had an opportunity to spend 5+ years in Bangalore. Talking about me, I am a very creative and dynamic individual always looking for something challenging and different to do which explains my participation in various activities from acting to Krav Maga. I enjoy working and collaborating with individuals who are excited about their fields and always ready to take up a challenge. Constantly troubled with vivid dreams and unexplored opportunities, I am looking to connect with anybody with a strong expertise in any field and would enjoy my company on our journey to scale it to reach a massive audience.
